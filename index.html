Culture

Hong Kong has a rich and diverse culture, blending traditional Chinese heritage with modern influences. Some key cultural aspects include:

  • Festivals: Chinese New Year, Mid-Autumn Festival, Dragon Boat Festival.
  • Food: Dim sum, street food, fusion cuisine.
  • Arts: Cantonese opera, contemporary art galleries, traditional crafts.
  • Landmarks: Victoria Harbour, The Peak, Tian Tan Buddha.

Essentials

Basic information for living in Hong Kong:

  • Transportation: Efficient public transport system including MTR, buses, and ferries.
  • Healthcare: High-quality public and private healthcare facilities.
  • Education: Numerous international schools and higher education institutions.
  • Accommodation: Variety of housing options from serviced apartments to local flats.
